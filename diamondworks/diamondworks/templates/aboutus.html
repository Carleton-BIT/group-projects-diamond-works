About Us

CUeats works with restaurants around Ottawa to bring Carleton students access to special deals and discounts. Our goal is to make it easier to find good, affordable food without having to spend too much time or money.

Our Goals

  • Help Carleton students save money on food
  • Work with more restaurants across Ottawa
  • Make student deals easy to find and use
  • Create a platform students can trust

Why We Matter

Eating out can get expensive, especially as a student. CUeats helps by providing places where you can save at popular spots across the city. Whether you’re near campus or out with friends, we help you find better options for less.

How It Works

Scan your discount code at participating restaurants to redeem your deal. Each semester, new deals are added as we partner with more restaurants across Ottawa. Deals can be used once per day, so you can keep coming back and saving.
