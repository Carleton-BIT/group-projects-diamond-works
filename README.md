## **Application Description:** 
CU-Eats is a web application designed to help Carleton students find discounted meals from restaurants in Ottawa. The platform is designed for Carleton students, who will be able to create an account using student authentication, log in, browse restaurants, search for food options, and view restaurant details and discounts. The application will make it easier for students to find affordable food options while also connecting them with local restaurants that offer student deals. Our long-term goal is to grow our platform till it becomes widely used by students, partner with multiple restaurants and franchises, and help students save money on food.
